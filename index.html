3rd Kovai cricket league for the deaf

Coimbatore District Association for the Deaf hosts the 3rd Kovai Cricket League for the Deaf-2025, a prestigious athletic event that uses cricket to empower the deaf population and promote inclusivity. This competition brings together skilled deaf cricket players from different areas, providing them with a professional stage on which to showcase their abilities, sportsmanship, and teamwork. The occasion serves as evidence of the Association's continued dedication to using the power of sports to promote inclusion in society and provide opportunities for people with disabilities.

About CDAD

Established in 1981, the Coimbatore District Association for the Deaf (CDAD) has been a beacon of support and empowerment for the deaf community in Coimbatore. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for individuals who are deaf or hard of hearing through advocacy, education, and community engagement.

Join us in making a difference! Whether you're looking to volunteer, donate, or participate in our events, there are many ways to get involved with CDAD. Together, we can create a more inclusive and supportive community for the deaf.
